# 🧾 AI Crypto Tax Assistant

**AI Crypto Tax Assistant** is an intelligent, fully automated cryptocurrency tax management tool designed for traders and investors in **the USA and Europe**. It tracks, calculates, and reports your crypto gains, losses, staking rewards, DeFi earnings, NFT transactions, and airdrops with full compliance to local tax regulations.  

Whether you're a casual investor or a professional trader, this assistant makes crypto taxation effortless, accurate, and fully optimized.

---

## 🚀 Features

### **1. Multi-Jurisdiction Support**
- **USA:** Full support for IRS regulations, including **capital gains, short-term vs long-term**, and reporting forms (Form 8949, Schedule D).  
- **Europe:** Country-specific rules for Germany, France, Spain, Netherlands, and more. Handles **long-term holdings, income from staking, DeFi, NFTs, and airdrops**.  
- Automatic country detection based on user profile or IP.

### **2. Automatic Transaction Import**
- Connect directly to **CEX platforms** (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Bitstamp, etc.) via API.  
- Support for **DEX & DeFi transactions** (Uniswap, PancakeSwap, Aave, Lido).  
- Import from **NFT marketplaces** (OpenSea, LooksRare).  
- Support for **hardware & software wallets** (Metamask, Ledger, Trezor).

### **3. Accurate Tax Calculations**
- Converts all crypto transactions into **fiat value at the transaction timestamp**.  
- Supports **capital gains, staking rewards, yield farming, token swaps, and NFT sales**.  
- Handles complex DeFi transactions and cross-chain transfers.  
- Supports **FIFO/LIFO/Specific Identification** accounting methods.

### **4. Tax Reports & Documentation**
- Generates **IRS-compliant forms** for USA: Form 8949, Schedule D, and 1040 summaries.  
- Produces **EU-ready PDF/CSV reports** for local tax authorities.  
- Detailed, line-by-line transaction history for auditing or bookkeeping.

### **5. Advanced Tax Optimization**
- Suggests **holding periods** to reduce short-term gains.  
- Optimizes trade timing for **maximum tax efficiency**.  
- Accounts for **foreign tax credits** and prevents double taxation.

### **6. Real-Time Notifications & Guidance**
- Alerts on **taxable events in real-time** (sales, swaps, staking rewards, NFT transactions).  
- Pre-trade notifications: “This transaction will create $X short-term gain.”  
- Built-in AI chat for instant guidance on complex scenarios.

### **7. Security & Privacy**
- All sensitive data is **encrypted** locally or in secure cloud storage.  
- **Private keys are never stored** without explicit user permission.  
- Optional **Zero-Knowledge Proofs** for privacy-preserving operations.

### **8. Accounting & Integration**
- Export data to **QuickBooks, Xero, or local accounting software**.  
- Provides **API endpoints** for tax advisors or custom dashboards.

### **9. User-Friendly Interface**
- Intuitive dashboard with **portfolio visualization, gains/losses charts, and filters by year, token, or transaction type**.  
- Mobile-ready: scan QR wallets or manually add transactions on the go.

## 💡 How It Works

1. **Transaction Collection:** Automatically gather all crypto transactions from exchanges, wallets, and DeFi platforms.
2. **Fiat Conversion:** Calculate the exact USD/EUR value at the time of each transaction.
3. **Tax Calculation:** Apply local regulations for capital gains, income, staking, airdrops, and NFTs.
4. **Report Generation:** Produce detailed and IRS/EU-compliant reports.
5. **Optimization & Alerts:** Suggest tax-efficient strategies and notify users of taxable events in real-time.
